Ad Angle Extractor (AAE).

Uncover winning ad angles in seconds. Analyzes top-performing ads across niches to identify messaging strategies.

Category

Web Tool

Role

AI & Marketing Automation

Tech Stack

VueJS, Laravel, Tailwind, AI

The Challenge

Marketers spend countless hours manually scrolling, capturing, and analyzing competitor ads in an attempt to find winning angles. This reliance on guesswork in ad copy creation often leads to low engagement, wasted budgets, and sluggish campaign launches.

The Solution

We built Ad Angle Extractor (AAE) to demystify high-converting marketing campaigns using artificial intelligence.

  • Modern Full-Stack: Crafted using a robust Laravel backend coupled with a highly interactive VueJS and DaisyUI frontend.
  • Intelligent Scraping: A specialized crawler continuously pulls the hottest ad creatives from major social networks.
  • AI Integration: Advanced Language Models dissect the creatives, automatically isolating psychological hooks, emotions, and core messaging themes.

The Impact

10x Faster Copywriting
3x Ad Conv. Rate
